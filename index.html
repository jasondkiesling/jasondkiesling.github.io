About Me

I'm Jason! I'm a software engineer in Lowell, MA who works remotely for RevOps. I graduated from the University of Massachusetts Lowell in December 2020 as a computer science major with minors in math and business. I'm an Eagle Scout and an active volunteer in the Boy Scouts of America. Much of my free time is spent doing web development for the Order of the Arrow - Scouting's National Honor Society - as the National Web Coordinator for their National Web Team.

My primary expertise is in full-stack engineering but I always enjoy learning something new. Outside of programming, I enjoy hiking and backpacking, cooking and baking, and spending time with friends and family.
